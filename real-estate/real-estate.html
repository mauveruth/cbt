MISSION

To create entrepreneurs in solar and CCTV sales, installation, repairs and to engage in collective cooperative investment in the provision of solar energy to the populace.

 

VISION

To make renewable energy available to the Nigerian population and Africa.

HIGHLIGHTS

Free training on solar panel installation and services.

Become a solar products distributor at no extra cost.

Learn installation and repair of inverters.

Get your complete home solar kits free.

Attract a solar project in your community for free.

Get a guaranteed short term cooperative loan for your business without collateral.

HOW TO PARTICIPATE

STEP ONE

Register as a member/distributor with #5000. Payment should be made by transfer or direct to the account of Heartland Multipurpose Cooperative Society Ltd, Zenith Bank Plc, Account Number: 1012250553.

STEP TWO

Send your payment details to: heartlandinvestment@gmail.com OR via text message or whatsapp to 08033058892. You will receive a token (epin) sent to your phone.

STEP THREE

Go to www.heartlandcooperative.com and click register. Then use the Registration epin and fill the form online.
